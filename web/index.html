Claude Desktop Buddy — Cheap Yellow Display

ESP32-2432S024 (2.4" 240×320 touch). Flash straight from your browser — no tools to install.

Plug the board into USB, then:

Your browser can't flash this — use desktop Chrome or Edge. This page must be served over HTTPS.

Chrome/Edge on desktop only. When prompted, pick the board's serial port (CP2102 / CH340). Installing replaces whatever is on the device.

After flashing

  1. In the Claude desktop app: Help → Troubleshooting → Enable Developer Mode.
  2. Developer → Open Hardware Buddy…, click Connect, pick Claude-XXXX.
  3. Grant Bluetooth permission. Enter the 6-digit passkey shown on the device.

Controls (touch)

This board has no physical buttons — the bottom bar is two touch zones: A (left) and B (right). Tap anywhere to wake; long-press A for the menu. On a permission prompt, A approves and B denies.

Firmware source & protocol: claude-desktop-buddy. The BLE API is a maker/developer feature, not an officially supported product.
